About us

Safety Boss Limited is a medium-sized company specializing in job safety products. We offer a wide range of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), harnesses, jobsite lighting, and more to ensure the safety of workers.

With over a decade of experience, we have become one of the most dominant businesses in the safety products sector. Our mission is to provide high-quality safety solutions to businesses in Trinidad & Tobago and other Caribbean territories.
